The Divergent Enterprise.

The Divergent Enterprise is a leading health and beauty franchise committed to producing, sustaining and marketing the highest quality of natural-based products, using certified organic and safe ingredients. Founded in 2013, the Divergent Enterprise has risen from the confines of a one-bedroom apartment to a multinational company, championing businesses that range from logistics and travel to health and beauty, including brands that have taken center stage in Nigeria and across Africa. Our success is driven by our staff and their commitment to creativity, breaking barriers and going the extra mile. We excel by operating responsibly, executing with excellence, applying innovative ideas and capturing new opportunities for growth and revenue generation.

Slimtea

Slimtea is a passionate health and weight loss brand that strives to promote an active, healthy and balanced lifestyle by combining certified all-natural weight loss ingredients such as green tea, cassia seeds, mulberry leaves and hemp kernels to create a fat-burning tea.
With thousands of customers globally, Slimtea has successfully made known and provided an effective, easy and sustainable route to weight loss, while promoting habits for a healthy lifestyle and not just a short-term fix. Popular Nigerian and African celebrities like Ini Edo, Annie Idibia and Huddah, have previously partnered with Slimtea, to promote the message of healthy living as brand ambassadors.

Who Are We.

We are one of Nigeria’s leading Ecommerce companies. We explore for, produce and transport authentic health and beauty products; market and distribute; generate job opportunities; invest in profitable new enterprises and solutions; and develop resourceful ideas for the future, including new ways to market our many brands.

Our Process.

We use every opportunity to think differently about strategies for new and better solutions for how we all serve our consumers – not just a few or the largest segment, but all of them. We all share very common needs, and when we begin to focus on what they are, we are able to identify connection points among people, rather than differences between them.

Our Approach.

Our approach can be summed up by one phrase: Shared Ambition, True Results. We believe that we are all connected by our aspirations to live better, healthier, longer and more purposeful lives.

Our Goal.

As retailers and brands, we aim to serve consumers in a much more meaningful and relevant way across product development, merchandising and marketing.

Our Mission.

We believe that as manufacturers and brands, we have an extraordinary opportunity to shift how we approach and serve all of our consumers, and as a result, reshape the health and beauty landscape from one of standardized ideals to one of realistic representations.
